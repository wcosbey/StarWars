Lets Go Fishing

Instructions

The bite is on!
Each time you click on one of the trophy game fish, a specific amount of points will be added to your total score.
You can hit your daily catch limit by matching your total catch to the daily catch limits number. You will lose the game if your total score goes above daily catch limit number.
The value of each trophy game fish is hidden from you until you click on one of them.
Each time this wicked game starts, the values of each trophy game fish radically change.
